Goal of the Survey:

The main goal of this research is to acquire and implement suitable empirical research techniques through an analysis of the influence of programming paradigms on development speed. As a result, you will be given a designated coding task to solve using JavaScript, focusing on either the Functional Programming or Object-Oriented Programming paradigm.

Rest assured that all personal information collected during this survey will be kept confidential and used for research purposes only. Your personal details will not be disclosed to any third party.
